Pakistan Culture

Our culture is very friendly to all people.

In Pakistan, respect and politeness are woven into daily life.
Guests are considered a blessing ("mehman nawazi"), offered chai (tea) and a seat of honor.
On streets, a simple "excuse me" (uzr hai) smooths the way. Helping a stranger is seen as good karma.
This tapestry of courtesy creates a welcoming atmosphere, a true taste of Pakistani warmth.
